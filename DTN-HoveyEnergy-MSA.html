01 Definitions
1.1
"AI Voice Agent" means the conversational artificial intelligence system, including associated telephony infrastructure, outbound calling workflows, voice scripts, and supporting configurations, built and operated by DTN under this Agreement.
1.2
"Call Content" means any script, messaging, promotional claim, telephony number, call list, or verbal representation delivered through the AI Voice Agent on behalf of Client.
1.3
"Deliverables" means all work product specifically developed for Client under a Statement of Work, including agent configurations, landing pages, dashboards, and reporting outputs.
1.4
"DTN Platform" means DTN's proprietary AI infrastructure, code, frameworks, integrations, methodologies, and know-how, including all third-party tools licensed by DTN (ElevenLabs, telephony APIs, CRM connectors, etc.).
1.5
"Go-Live Date" means the date on which the AI Voice Agent completes successful end-to-end testing and is deployed for live outbound calling, as confirmed in writing by DTN to Client.
1.6
"Monthly Recurring Services" means the ongoing AI agent hosting, maintenance, call management, and active optimization services provided after Go-Live under Phase 2 of the SOW.
1.7
"Regulatory Requirements" means all applicable federal, state, and local laws, rules, and regulations governing outbound telephone communications, including without limitation the Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA), the Telemarketing Sales Rule (TSR), Do-Not-Call Registry requirements, Illinois Public Utilities Act, and all applicable Public Utilities Commission (PUC) regulations.
1.8
"Statement of Work" or "SOW" means the project specification attached as Exhibit A to this Agreement (DTN-2026-004), which defines scope, deliverables, milestones, and fees.
02 Scope of Services
2.1
Services. DTN shall provide the services described in the SOW (Exhibit A), which is incorporated herein by reference. In the event of any conflict between this Agreement and the SOW, this Agreement controls unless the SOW explicitly states otherwise.

DTN shall design, configure, and deploy the AI Voice Agent system, including: AI agent script design and compliance review; telephony infrastructure setup; lead list acquisition and CRM integration; a 100-call pilot program; dashboard and reporting; and initial team training. Usage exceeding 2,000 minutes per month will be billed at DTN's then-current overage rate.
2.4
Change Orders. Any material change to the scope of services requires a written change order signed by both Parties. DTN is not obligated to perform out-of-scope work pending execution of a change order.
2.5
Client Responsibilities. Client shall: (a) designate a primary point of contact; (b) provide timely access to all information, approvals, and materials reasonably required by DTN; (c) ensure all phone numbers provided by Client are compliant with applicable DNC and TCPA requirements; and (d) respond to DTN review requests within five (5) business days. Delays attributable to Client's failure to perform its obligations may extend timelines without liability to DTN.

4.4
