Built on our Triple A Approach — Aim, Architecture, and Accountability — every session is designed to produce results your team can use the next day.

Week 1 — Build
D1
Phase 1 · Aim

Discovery & Planning

Before we build anything, we align on what winning looks like. DTN works directly with your sales leadership to audit your team's current workflow, map the tools in your stack, and pinpoint the two or three AI opportunities that will move the needle fastest.

  • ✓ Deep-dive with sales leadership on goals and gaps
  • ✓ Full audit of tools, CRM, and current workflows
  • ✓ Identify highest-ROI AI opportunities for your specific team
  • ✓ Define success metrics before Day 2 begins
  • ✓ Custom training blueprint delivered to leadership
D2–3
Phase 2 · Architecture

The Team Workshop

This is where the upgrade begins. The full team comes together — in-person or virtual — and builds real AI agents for real workflows. Not demos. Not theory. Reps leave with something that works the next morning.

  • ✓ Hands-on agent building for lead research, outreach, and CRM logging
  • ✓ Call prep, objection handling, and follow-up automation
  • ✓ Prompt engineering basics — reps learn to think in AI, not just use it
  • ✓ Available in-person or virtually, based on your team's needs
D4–5
Phase 2 · Architecture Continued

1-on-1 Agent Setup

Every rep sells differently. These individual sessions dial in each person's AI workflow to their specific territory, tools, and selling style. No one gets left behind, and no one is stuck with a generic setup.

  • ✓ Personal sessions with each team member
  • ✓ Agents configured to individual workflow, territory, and tools
  • ✓ Each rep ends the week with a fully working AI setup
Week 2 — Reinforce
D6–8

Accountability Coaching

Real adoption happens in week two. We return for individual coaching sessions to troubleshoot what's not working, refine what is, and layer in new agents as confidence grows. This is where most training programs stop — we're just getting started.
