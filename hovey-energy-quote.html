Project Quote &
Terms of Engagement

This document outlines the project scope, pricing, payment schedule, and terms for the AI Voice Agent engagement between DTN Consulting ("DTN") and Hovey Energy ("Client"). A detailed Scope of Work will be provided separately for collaborative review.

Project Overview

What DTN Is Building

DTN will build, deploy, and manage a production AI outbound voice agent purpose-built for Hovey Energy's sales operation. The system is designed to replace the workload of two fronters, operating as a completely independent calling system alongside Hovey's existing outbound dialer.

  • AI Voice Agent
    One (1) AI voice agent configured for Hovey Energy's ICP, qualification logic, objection handling, and live transfer workflow. The agent is licensed to run continuously during configured operating hours with no limit on the number of leads processed.
  • Cloud Hosted
    The system is fully cloud hosted and internet dependent. No hardware, servers, or on-premise infrastructure are required from Hovey Energy. An internet connection is the only technical requirement.
  • Independent System
    The AI voice agent operates independently from Hovey's existing outbound dialer. The two systems do not interact. Hovey's current team workflow remains unchanged.
  • Dashboard & Reporting
    A custom metrics dashboard providing call outcome tracking, performance reporting, and lead status visibility. Full MP3 recording and transcription of every call.
  • Same Team, Start to Finish
    The DTN team that builds the system is the same team that deploys, manages, and optimizes it. No handoffs to a support desk or third party.

Terms

Agreement
  • 1. Effective Date
    This agreement becomes effective on the date both parties sign below (the "Effective Date"). DTN will invoice Milestone 1 on the Effective Date. Project work commences upon receipt of Milestone 1 payment.
  • 2. Agent License & Usage
    The monthly managed services fee includes the right to operate one (1) AI voice agent for up to two thousand (2,000) minutes of voice agent usage per calendar month. The agent may run continuously during configured operating hours, and Client may feed the agent as many leads as desired within the monthly minute allowance. Usage beyond 2,000 minutes per month, additional concurrent agents, or expanded capacity will be quoted separately and agreed upon in writing at the time of scaling.
  • 3. Hosting & Infrastructure
    The AI voice agent system is fully cloud hosted and managed by DTN. No hardware, server, or on-premise infrastructure is required from Client. System availability is dependent on internet connectivity and third-party cloud service uptime. DTN does not guarantee specific uptime percentages but will use commercially reasonable efforts to maintain system availability.
  • 4. Monthly Services Start Date
    Monthly managed services fees begin on the Go-Live date, defined as the date DTN delivers the production-ready system to Client. No monthly fees are charged during the project build phase.
  • 5. Monthly Services Scope
    The monthly fee includes three (3) days of dedicated specialist time per month, plus all AI platform, telephony, and API costs for up to 2,000 minutes of voice agent usage per month. Work requested by Client beyond three (3) days in any calendar month, or usage beyond 2,000 minutes per month, will be billed at a rate agreed upon in writing prior to the work being performed.
  • 6. Monthly Cancellation
    Client may cancel monthly managed services at any time with thirty (30) days written notice. No minimum commitment period applies to monthly services. Cancellation of monthly services does not affect project fee obligations.
  • 7. Compliance Responsibility
    DTN will configure the AI voice agent with compliance-supporting features including do-not-call list management, AI disclosure language, opt-out handling, and call log retention. Client is responsible for ensuring its use of the AI voice agent complies with all applicable federal, state, and local laws and regulations, including but not limited to TCPA, state telemarketing laws, and do-not-call requirements. DTN recommends Client consult qualified legal counsel regarding applicable regulations prior to Go-Live. DTN is not liable for any fines, penalties, or legal actions arising from Client's use of the system.
  • 8. Intellectual Property
    All call recordings, transcriptions, and lead data generated by the system are the property of Client. Prompt configurations, AI agent architecture, system code, and proprietary tooling developed by DTN remain the intellectual property of DTN Consulting. Client receives a non-exclusive license to use the system for the duration of the managed services engagement.
  • 9. Confidentiality
    Both parties agree to treat all information shared during this engagement as confidential and will not disclose it to third parties without prior written consent. This includes but is not limited to business strategies, customer data, call recordings, system configurations, and pricing.
  • 10. Limitation of Liability
    DTN's total liability under this agreement shall not exceed the total fees paid by Client to DTN under this agreement. DTN shall not be liable for any indirect, incidental, consequential, or punitive damages, including but not limited to lost revenue, lost profits, or business interruption, regardless of the cause of action. The AI voice agent is provided as a managed service; DTN does not guarantee specific sales outcomes, lead conversion rates, or revenue results.
  • 11. Termination for Cause
    Either party may terminate this agreement with written notice if the other party materially breaches any term of this agreement and fails to cure such breach within fifteen (15) days of receiving written notice of the breach.
  • 12. Governing Law
    This agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Illinois, without regard to conflict of law principles.
