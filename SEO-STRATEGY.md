## On-Page SEO Implementation

### Homepage (index.html)

**Title Tag:**
```html
<title>AI Sales Enablement Training | DTN Consulting</title>
```

**Meta Description:**
```html
<meta name="description" content="Train your enterprise sales team to build AI agents that automate busywork. Former sales leaders teaching vibe coding for CRM automation, meeting prep, and pipeline management.">
```

**Keywords Meta:**
```html
<meta name="keywords" content="AI sales enablement, sales AI training, AI sales assistant, enterprise sales automation, vibe coding, sales team AI, CRM automation, B2B sales AI">
```

**H1 Tag:**
```html
<h1>Build at the Speed of Thought.</h1>
```
(Consider changing to: "Train Your Sales Team to Build AI Agents")

### Structured Data (JSON-LD)
Added to homepage for rich search results:

```json
{
  "@type": "ProfessionalService",
  "name": "DTN Consulting",
  "description": "AI Sales Enablement Training...",
  "serviceType": ["AI Sales Enablement Training", ...]
}
```
