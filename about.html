Our Executive Team

Steve Sassi

Managing Partner & Principal Transformation Strategist

Steve Sassi is the Managing Partner & Principal Transformation Strategist at DTN Consulting, bringing nearly two decades of enterprise growth and go-to-market strategy experience. His journey in tech sales began in 2005, helping enterprises enhance digital document workflows, and has since evolved into leading complex digital and cloud transformations for Fortune 500 organizations.

Recognized repeatedly as a top global sales performer, Steve has engineered multi-million-dollar AI, cloud, and data strategies at industry giants like Informatica and Pivotree. He specializes in aligning AI-powered data solutions with enterprise cloud platforms including Azure, Google Cloud, and AWS.

At DTN Consulting, Steve leverages his extensive background to help businesses build robust, data-driven cultures. He is passionate about guiding clients through the intricacies of new customer acquisition, pipeline expansion, and leveraging cutting-edge AI methodologies to drive unprecedented revenue growth.

Nick Bianco

Partner & Technical Advisor

Nick Bianco serves as Partner and Technical Advisor, leveraging over 15 years of expertise as a results-driven Enterprise IT Executive. With a strong foundation established during his tenure at IBM and its spin-off, Merative, Nick has a proven track record of driving innovation in enterprise systems, cloud-based solutions, and AI across complex landscapes.

Beyond his corporate leadership, Nick is deeply fascinated by hands-on technical architecture, which gives him a unique, ground-level perspective. His personal immersion in Generative AI, homelab design, virtualization (Proxmox), and app development allows him to engage intimately with how cloud-native platforms and AI intersect to transform enterprise workflows.

An entrepreneurial spirit at his core, Nick is also the Co-Founder of Spiritus Coffee Co., a mission-driven specialty brand. This venture perfectly encapsulates his ability to blend modern eCommerce fulfillment and operations with a profound commitment to community impact, mental health support, and workforce development.
